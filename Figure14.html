Edmund Barton

Edmund Barton was born on the 18th of January 1849 and died the 7th of January 1920. Edmund was born to his father William and mother Mary Louise, he was born in the state of New South Wales in the suburb of Glebe. He attended the primary school of Sydney Grammar School then proceeded to attend Fort Street High School, then to pursue his education and go to University Of Sydney. In 1877 he got married to Jane Mason Ross. As a couple they had 6 children (Edmund Alfred, Wilfrid Alexander, Jean Alice, Arnold Hubert, Oswald, and Leila Stephanie.) During 1871 he started working as a barista. He worked in the office (prime minister) from 1901-1903. On the 7th of January 1920 Edmund Barton passed away due to congestive heart failure.
