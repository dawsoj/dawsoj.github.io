Salvador del solar

Salvador del solar is Peru’s prime minister he was born on May 1st 1970. Salvador graduated university as a lawyer but Salvador is also an actor, in 1999 he acted in a film called Pantaleon and the film was a success, in that same year he stared in Pobre Diabla the film was such a success that it was exported to the UK and USA. On March 11th 2019 he became president.
