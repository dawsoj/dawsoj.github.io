Vladimir Putin

Vladimir Putin is the current president of Russia. He was born on the 7th of October 1952. He has been president of Russia from 2000 - 2008 and was re-elected in 2012. Before Vladimir was president he was a KGB officer which translated into English means committee for state security.

"Enemies are right in front of you, you are at war with them, then you make an armistice with them, and all is clear. A traitor must be destroyed, crushed."
