Omar al-Bashir

After being born in 1944, Omar al-Bashir studied at a military college then fought in 1973 with the Egyptian army. After returning to Sudan and receiving a series of promotions in the 1980s, he started a political career and in 1994 he was elected President of Sudan and served until 2019
