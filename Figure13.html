Sergio Mattarella

Sergio Mattarella was born on the 23rd of July 1941. He was elected as president of Italy on the 3rd of February 2015. Sergio is also Italy's 12th president. He also served as minister of defence between 1999 and 2001.

"I had no intention of entering politics, but then the force of events led me to become involved in politics."
