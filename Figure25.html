Miguel Diaz-Canel

Miguel Diaz-Canel is the president of Cuba. Migal was born on the 20th of April 1960, Miguel is the 17th president. Before he was vice president of Cuba from 2013-2018. Miguel served as a minister of high education from 2009-2012. He was promoted in that same year to deputy prime minister. A year later he became the first vice president. Miguel has two children with his wife Martha but now has a second wife called Lis Cuesta.
