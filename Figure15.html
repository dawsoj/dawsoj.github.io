Alfred Deakin

Alfred Deakin was born on the 3rd Of August and died on the 7th Of October 1919. Alfred’s parents names were William and Sarah and they gave birth to him in Fitzroy (Melbourne). Originally Alfred’s law career turned out to be unsuccessful, although in the end he served Australia for 3 terms (1903-1904, 1905-1908, 1909-1910) During his 2nd term it was obvious the amount of effort he was putting in was affecting his health, he wrote in his private journals that he was starting to lose his memory.On the 7th of October 1919 Alfred passed away due to Meningoencephalitis.
