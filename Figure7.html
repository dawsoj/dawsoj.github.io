William Wallace

William Wallace was a knight from Scotland in the late 12th century. He was a leader in the first war of Scottish independence. William was born in 1270 and died 23rd of August 1305. He is one of Scotland's greatest heroes.

"We come here with no peaceful intent, but ready for battle, determined to avenge our wrongs and set our country free. Let your masters come and attack us: we are ready to meet them beard to beard."
