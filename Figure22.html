Justin Trudeau

Justin Trudeau is the Canadian president. He was born on December 25th 1971 (aged 47). Justin is the 23rd Canadian president, he was elected in 2013 and was the leader of the liberal party in 2015. Justin was the second youngest president to be elected (right after Joe Clark).

"The North American idea that diversity is strength, is our great gift to the world. No matter where you are from, or the faith you profess, nor the colour of your skin, nor whom you love, you belong here. This is home."
