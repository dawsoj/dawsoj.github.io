Abdel Fattah el-Sisi

Abdel Fattah el-Sisi is the President of Egypt and was born in 1954. He got married to Entissar Amer in 1977 well before he went to US Army War College in 2006 as his education. In 1977 after his education at Egypt Military Academy he served as an infantryman. In 2014 he was elected President of Egypt.

"The Egyptian people have free will. they choose whoever they want to rule them. The Army and the Police now are careful for people's will to choose their rulers."
