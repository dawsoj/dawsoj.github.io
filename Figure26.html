Ram Nath Kovind

The current President of India, Ram Nath Kovind, was born in October 1945. He grew up in a humble village and studied commerce and law at Kanpur University. His father Maikulal Kori owned and small-time grocery store. He worked as a lawyer before starting in politics and then married Savita Kovind in 1974 before becoming president in 2017.
