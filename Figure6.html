Xi Jinping

Born in 1953, Xi Jinping climbed the vigourously party ranks. He was then elected as a Secretary for the Communist party after many disastrous attempts. He got married in 1987 and was divorced a few years later. In 1987 he married Peng Liyuan, a folk singer. In March 2013 he was elected President of China.

"It was the greatest contribution towards the whole of human race, made by China, is to prevent its 1.3 billion people from hunger."
